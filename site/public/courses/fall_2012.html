|
 Philosophy of Language
|
 TD-W 2600 | (MWF 10:00am-10:50am) + Discussion

Introduction to philosophical problems and theories concerning the nature of language. Topics typically include the notion of linguistic structure, theories of meaning and reference, names and descriptions, the relations between languages and thought, necessity and analytic truty, and conversational norms.

Philosophy 150E/250E
|
 Advanced Metaphysics
|
 PHELP 2524 | (MWF 12:00pm-12:50pm)

This is a course about the metaphysics of personal identity. Little Suzy is eating cake at her 4th birthday party; 16 years later, Suzanne is taking an organic chemistry exam at UCSB. It is a fact, we will suppose, that Little Suzy is the same person as college student Suzanne? What makes it the case that this is a fact? What does this fact consist in?
